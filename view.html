Edward Kessy

Principal Investment Facilitation Officer with National Economic Empowerment Council

Principal Investment Facilitation Officer with National Economic Empowerment Council under Prime Minister’s Office, United Republic of Tanzania. Edward has over ten years experience working in both reputable private and public institutions in Tanzania. Advocate of entrepreneurship ecosystem development as he believes Small and Medium Enterprises is the life line of the developing world economy as the most African countries aspire becoming a middle income country by 2025. He holds an MBA in Marketing from Open University of Tanzania, Bachelor of Business Administration (Marketing) from Mzumbe University Tanzania, Bachelor of Laws from Open University of Tanzania and Post Graduate Diploma in Legal Practice from Law School of Tanzania and Advocate of Tanzania High Court and Courts Subordinate thereto save for Primary Court.
